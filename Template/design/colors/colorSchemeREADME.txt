--Naming description--
cs - b - i - 1
{1}-{2}-{3}-{4}

{1} - color-scheme
{2} - entity of color scheme
{3} - entity modifier (optional)
{4} - color-scheme entity number

--Entities list--
t    - text
l    - link
g    - gradient
bg   - background
br   - border
svg  - svg

--Entity modifier list--

i    - interactive (ex. cs-bg-i-1 as colorsheme-background-interactive-1) It says that color changed on hover.
ds   - dashed Add dashed border-bottom to link
d    - dotted Add dashed border-bottom to link
